About Me

My name is James Dansie, and I'm a software developer. Prior to software development I was a research engineer, where I worked on industrial combustion and low pollution technologies. I took new ideas, and made products out of them. I helped to bring those new products to market, and helped to solve their products as they arose. However, I was highly specialized. I felt pigeon holed and stagnant. I was using python at work to sort out experimental data, and I enjoyed that more than my actual work, so I decided to make it my career. I have absolutely loved going back to school and to start learning again! It feels great. I am looking for a position where I can continue learn and grow.
